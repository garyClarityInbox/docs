## Train Clarity to learn about your business

Clarity Inbox learns from past replies and future replies. Additionally, Clarity Inbox can learn through Knowledge Items.

**Add Knowledge Items**

1. Visit the [Clarity Inbox Dashboard](https://app.clarityinbox.com/dashboard) \> Guidance \> Content 
2. Click Add Knowledge 
3. Fill out the Title 
4. Fill out the Description. 
5. Click Save.
