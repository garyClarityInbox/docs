## Teach Clarity your CC rules

1. **Visit **\
   Visit the Clarity Inbox Dashboard > Guidance > CC Rules
2. **CC**\
   Clarity auto-labels and prioritizes emails, so you can focus on what matters and respond quickly.

Common Use Case #1:
Any email about investors, CC teammate.
