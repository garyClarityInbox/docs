## Add Meeting Links (Clarity, Cal.com, Google Meet, etc.)

**Enable**\
   Visit the Clarity Inbox Dashboard > Guidance > Meeting Links
   Clarity will recall this meeting link in creating the draft options.
   Click Enable.
   Click Save.
