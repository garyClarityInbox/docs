## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How do I get started with my first playbook?">
  Choose the category that addresses your biggest email pain point, browse the available playbooks, and follow the copy-paste setup instructions. Most playbooks take 2-5 minutes to configure.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I use multiple playbooks at the same time?">
  Absolutely! Each playbook handles different email types and scenarios. Many founders use 3-5 playbooks together to create a complete email automation system.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if I need to customize a playbook for my specific needs?">
  Every playbook template is fully editable. You can adjust the AI rules, email templates, CC recipients, and labels to match your workflow and brand voice.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do playbooks integrate with my existing tools?">
  Playbooks work with Gmail, Outlook, and can integrate with your CRM, Slack, Zapier, and other tools through BCCs, webhooks, and automation platforms.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if a playbook makes a mistake?">
  All playbooks create drafts for your review before sending. You maintain full control and can edit, customize, or delete any automated response before it goes out.
</Accordion>
