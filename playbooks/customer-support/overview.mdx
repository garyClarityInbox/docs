## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="How do I ensure support tickets never slip through the cracks?">
  ClarityInbox uses AI to detect support-related keywords, auto-labels them, BCCs your support team, and archives messages—so every request is tracked in your help desk.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I customize routing rules for different support channels?">
  Yes. Create separate AI playbooks for bugs, billing issues, and feature requests with unique labels and destinations (e.g., Intercom, Zendesk, email aliases).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I keep my sales inbox free from support noise?">
  Use the routing playbook to archive support emails from your main inbox automatically, ensuring sales conversations remain focused and uncluttered.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What if a VIP customer needs immediate attention?">
  The VIP Escalation playbook flags VIP senders with high-priority labels, auto-CCs founders and leadership, and sends Slack or SMS alerts so your best customers are never left waiting.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I measure support performance and satisfaction?">
  Integrate with Zapier to log tickets, response times, and resolution outcomes in Google Sheets, Airtable, or your CRM. Use these metrics to optimize workflows and improve customer satisfaction.
</Accordion>
