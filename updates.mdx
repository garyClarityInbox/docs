# August 2025 Update

## 📝 Train Clarity in your voice

Clarity now learns your tone and style. You can guide it in [**Dashboard**](https://app.clarityinbox.com/dashboard)\*\* → Train → Guidance\*\*.

- Adjust tone of voice
- Greeting style & Sign-off signature
- Adding your Calendar link

Tired of looping in the same teammates? Clarity can now handle that automatically, so you never miss the right people on a thread.

## 📇 Contacts that remember everything

Clarity understands your relationships. In [**Dashboard**](https://app.clarityinbox.com/dashboard) → Train → Contacts, click a contact to see:

- Actions from latest threads for each relationship
- Drafted replies fine-tuned to that specific person
